AMO MBUTUMA

CO FOUNDER / PRODUCER Filmmaker | Production Manager/LP | Life Enthusiast
More Info

Passionate about bringing stories to life, I thrive in the world of film production. As a seasoned Production Manager, I've had the privilege to contribute to some of the industry's most exciting projects, including the critically acclaimed Silverton Siege (Netflix) and the groundbreaking international 13-part series, “The Professionals”—one of the biggest productions to ever hit our shores. I’m all about balancing hard work with a love for life, always eager to tackle challenges head-on. Whether it's coordinating high-stakes sets or building relationships on and off the camera, I bring my fun, hardworking, and people-focused energy to everything I do. I believe in principles, respect, and staying bold in the face of adversity. I'm a happy chap who loves pushing the limits of creativity and productivity. When I'm not behind the scenes making magic happen, you’ll find me diving into the world of cars or taking on whatever exciting challenge life throws my way.
